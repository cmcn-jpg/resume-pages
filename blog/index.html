About Me

We all want to make money, make a difference, change the future, and I decided that I can do all those things in computer science. I took a webpage design course my freshman year of highschool at Northwest PA Collegiate Academy, which I found to be useless and I learned next to nothing. For the next two years I did little to no coding, spending my time on other things. After leaving Collegiate at the end of my sophomore year, I attended Erie High School for my junior year during which i had signed up for a class that's name was something like CS Principles or video game design or some other generic title. The teacher of this course was a man named Jordan Lander, a higher level science teacher at the school. I didn't know what to expect of this course but I enjoyed my first few days of it. I dont recall quite when it was, but at some point towards the beginning of the course I took off and got way ahead. Eventually I wasn't even following the curriculum on code.org and Jordan Lander had recruited me for a special project in which i created Project Vibe. Project Vibe's goal was to produce a pre-diagnosis of specific mental conditons based on survey results. The website has since been shut down due to lack of funds to keep it open. Even at this point I wasn't as far as i would have liked.

Working at my own pace through online tutorials was the key for me and now I'm working on this blog to test my knowledge
